Hobbes had painted the state of nature as a **war of all against all**, in which naturally competitive, fearful humans, lacking a common power, live in perpetual danger, and life is "solitary, poor, nasty, brutish, and short." From this dark premise he derived the need for an absolute sovereign.

Rousseau attacks the premise at its root. Hobbes's savage, he argues, is not natural man at all but *social* man projected backward — a creature already corrupted by pride, competition, and the artificial desires that only society produces, then imagined to exist before society. Hobbes "spoke of savage man and described civil man."

Rousseau's own natural human is almost the opposite. In the true state of nature, humans are solitary but not hostile — simple, self-sufficient, wandering the forests, moved by two natural sentiments: *amour de soi*, a healthy self-preservation, and **pity**, an innate reluctance to see another suffer. Lacking language, foresight, property, and vanity, natural man has no reason for the wars Hobbes imagines; he is neither good nor evil but innocent, and on the whole peaceable.

If so, the Hobbesian conclusion collapses. Conflict, greed, and domination are not our natural condition, requiring an absolute sovereign to suppress; they are *products of society itself* — of the invention of property, the growth of inequality, and the birth of *amour-propre*, the corrosive need to compare oneself to others and be esteemed. It is civilization, not nature, that makes men wolves.

The critique reverses the political lesson. For Hobbes, our natural wickedness justifies unlimited power to keep the peace. For Rousseau, our natural goodness means the evils Hobbes fears are society's own creation — and the task is not to cage a beast but to construct, through the **general will**, a legitimate order that recovers freedom rather than merely imposing control. Where Hobbes reads the state of nature as an argument *for* Leviathan, Rousseau reads it as an indictment of the inequality and corruption that actual societies have produced.
