Carnap's early masterpiece, *The Logical Structure of the World* (the *Aufbau*), is in large part the attempt to carry out a program Russell had proposed — and Carnap said as much, taking Russell's slogan as his watchword: "wherever possible, logical constructions are to be substituted for inferred entities."

From Russell, Carnap inherited the twin tools that define his project: modern **symbolic logic** (the logic of *Principia Mathematica*) and the method of **logical construction**. Russell had suggested that physical objects, other minds, and the entities of science need not be posited as unknowable things inferred behind experience; instead they could be *constructed* as logical functions of what is actually given — sense-data. Rather than inferring matter, we build it, logically, out of the data. This was Russell's way of honoring empiricism while using the new logic.

Carnap takes this program and executes it with a rigor and system Russell only sketched. The *Aufbau* attempts an actual **step-by-step logical construction of the whole world of knowledge** — from a minimal basis in elementary experiences, using only logical relations, Carnap tries to define, in ascending order, perceptual objects, physical things, other minds, and cultural objects. It is Russell's "logical constructions" turned into a complete constitutional system of concepts.

Carnap combines this Russellian method with the empiricist demand that all meaningful concepts be traceable to experience and the logicist view of mathematics — fusing Russell's logic, Russell's constructionism, and the verificationism of the Vienna Circle into the program of **logical empiricism**.

So Carnap continues Russell as the systematizer who took Russell's method of logical construction and his mathematical logic and built from them the technical framework of logical positivism. Where Russell offered brilliant fragments and a guiding maxim — construct rather than infer — Carnap attempted the full, rigorous edifice. Through Carnap, the Russellian method became the working practice of analytic philosophy of science.
